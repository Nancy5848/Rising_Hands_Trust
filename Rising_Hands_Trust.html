About Rising Hands Trust

Building bridges of hope, creating pathways to prosperity

Founded in 2009, Rising Hands Trust emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where every individual has access to basic necessities, quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth. What started as a small community initiative has blossomed into a comprehensive social welfare organization touching thousands of lives across Punjab and beyond.

We are driven by the belief that sustainable change comes from empowering communities, not just providing aid. Through our holistic approach, we address root causes of poverty, inequality, and social injustice, creating lasting impact that reverberates through generations.

Our dedicated team of professionals, volunteers, and community partners work tirelessly to implement programs that are not only effective but also dignified, respecting the agency and potential of every individual we serve. We operate with complete transparency, ensuring that every rupee donated creates maximum impact.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities through comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, livelihood, and social development. We strive to create sustainable solutions that break the cycle of poverty and foster self-reliance, dignity, and hope for a brighter future.

👁️

Our Vision

A just and equitable society where every individual, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education, healthcare, nutritious food, and opportunities to realize their full potential. We envision communities that are self-sufficient, resilient, and thriving.

💎

Our Values

Compassion guides our actions, integrity defines our operations, transparency builds trust, innovation drives our solutions, and collaboration amplifies our impact. We believe in treating every beneficiary with dignity and respect, recognizing their inherent worth and potential.
